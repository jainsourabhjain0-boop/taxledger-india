GST Registration for Small Business: Step-by-Step Practical Guide

A straightforward GST registration guide for proprietors, freelancers and small businesses.

When registration matters

GST registration can become relevant based on turnover, nature of supply, interstate activity and other triggers. Some businesses also register voluntarily to claim input tax credit and improve credibility.

After registration

Once registered, invoice compliance, return filing, tax payment and record maintenance become equally important. Registration without process discipline creates future compliance issues.
