ITR Filing Checklist: Documents, Reconciliation and Common Mistakes to Avoid

20 June 2026 • Editorial Desk • 8 min read

A complete checklist for salaried taxpayers and small professionals before filing income tax return.

Ad placement block

Responsive in-article AdSense slot

Start with reconciliation

Before opening the return form, reconcile salary, interest income, capital gains, TDS, advance tax and deductions with Form 26AS, AIS/TIS, bank statements and supporting documents. Most notices arise from mismatch rather than complex tax issues.

Core documents to keep ready

Keep PAN, Aadhaar, bank account details, Form 16, Form 26AS, AIS/TIS, home loan statement, investment proofs, donation receipts, health insurance premium receipts and details of other income ready in one folder.

Watch for mismatch areas

Interest income, dividend income, securities transactions, multiple employers, old tax demands, foreign assets and omitted bank accounts are frequent trouble points. Review these carefully before submission.

For professionals and freelancers

Also reconcile gross receipts, expense records, TDS credits, GST turnover and bank receipts. If presumptive taxation is being considered, compare it with regular computation rather than assuming it is always beneficial.

After filing

Download the acknowledgement, verify the return in time, and keep a PDF set of all workings. If refund is expected, ensure pre-validation of bank account is complete.
