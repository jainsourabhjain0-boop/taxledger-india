Section 194J Explained: TDS on Professional Fees, Technical Services and More

A practical guide to section 194J covering applicability, threshold, rates, examples and compliance.

What is section 194J?

Section 194J requires deduction of tax at source on specified payments such as professional fees, technical fees, royalty and certain director remuneration. It becomes relevant when businesses make payments to consultants, professionals and technical service providers.

Compliance points

Track vendor-wise payments, deduct tax at the correct time, deposit within due date and report it in the proper TDS return. Reconcile expense ledger, vendor ledger and TDS return before filing to avoid mismatch.
