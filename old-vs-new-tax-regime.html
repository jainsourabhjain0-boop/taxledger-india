Old vs New Tax Regime: How to Choose the Better Option

A decision framework to compare old and new tax regime based on deductions and tax planning.

How to choose

Compare taxable income under both regimes, consider deductions like section 80C, 80D and HRA where relevant, and calculate actual tax instead of guessing.

Practical rule

Review the comparison whenever salary, bonus, rent, housing loan or investments change materially during the year.
